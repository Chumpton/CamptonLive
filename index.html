About Campton

I grew up in the special needs school system with my single mother. Living with Autism, ADHD, and severe bipolar personality disorder brought unique challenges, and I eventually dropped out of high school and stepped away from the traditional workforce to find my own path.

I decided to follow my heart and explore a personal spiritual journey. Through the support of plant medicines like cannabis and psilocybin, alongside ancient yogic practices and professional video gaming, I began to find my footing. Sharing what I learned online helped me connect with an amazing audience and build a sense of purpose outside the typical system.

Today, I'm the founder of The Microstead Method and the author of two books. I spend my time teaching, creating art, and dedicating myself to nature and initiatives that help protect our world.
